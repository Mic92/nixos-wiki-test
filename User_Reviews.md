This page list some blog entries, comments made by Nix / NixOS users or by other community members. The primary goal of this page is to track bad points and possible enhancements that can be done on these projects. This also provide us a way to analyse and improve our communication skills by adapting our talk to our audience.

Do not take the content of the following links as granted. Some texts are not indexed inside this page because they do not add any valuable feedback.
